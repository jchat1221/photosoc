About Us

We are the University of St Andrews Photography Society. As a student run society are hope is to connect students that share a love for photography. From portrait to landscape we hope to provide a platform where students can learn new skills. We run workshops including basics, wildlife and street photography. Every year we also run exhibitions where students can show their work. Check out our Facebook page for more information about workshops and events.
